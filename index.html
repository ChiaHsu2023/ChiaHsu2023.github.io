Chia Hsu

Hi! I am a PhD candidate fellow from Design Institute at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, and a researcher at the IUI Lab, luckily advised by Prof. Neng-Hao Yu. I also work as a product development researcher at Surreal Co., Ltd.

My research focuses on using MR and AI to enhancing language acquisition for EFL Children, with an emphasis on interaction design in MR environments.
